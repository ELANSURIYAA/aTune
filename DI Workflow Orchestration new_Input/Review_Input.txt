Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks

- Verify that all mandatory sections specified in the agent instructions are present in the output, including both “With available agents” and “With available and new agents” sections.
- Confirm that all required fields, tables, and headers outlined in the instructions are included in the output.
- Check that the output addresses all entities, components, and dependencies referenced in the instructions.
- Ensure that all required analyses, mappings, and business rules from the instructions are covered in the output.
- Validate that all formatting and structural requirements from the instructions are followed.
- Confirm that all mandatory classifications, tags, and statuses specified in the instructions are included.
- Ensure that all gap/missing item handling requirements from the instructions are addressed.
- Verify that all referenced files or content from the instructions are analyzed where required.
- Confirm that all required improvements, optimizations, and run metrics sections are present.
- Ensure that no mandatory section or requirement from the instructions is skipped.

Accuracy Checks

- Confirm that the output aligns strictly with the provided agent instructions and does not introduce unsupported assumptions.
- Verify that no hallucinated content or agent names are present in the output.
- Ensure that all dependency and technical mappings are logically and technically correct as per the instructions.
- Check that all findings, recommendations, and classifications are traceable to the instructions.
- Validate that terminology and formatting are used consistently and as specified in the instructions.
- Confirm that no contradictions or inconsistencies exist across sections of the output.
- Ensure that all business rule interpretations are correct and instruction-aligned.
- Verify that all required output structures, such as tables and bullet points, match the format and detail level specified in the instructions.
- Confirm that no false-positive gaps or false-negative omissions occur in the output.
- Ensure that all values, tables, and classifications accurately match the requirements in the instructions.

Efficiency Checks

- Verify that duplicate analysis, redundant explanations, and repeated gap entries are avoided in the output.
- Confirm that the output remains concise, well-structured, and free from unnecessary sections.
- Ensure that duplicate dependency mappings and repeated risks or recommendations are eliminated.
- Check that formatting is clean, optimized, and does not include excessive verbosity.
- Validate that overlapping findings are consolidated and similar findings are logically grouped where appropriate.
- Ensure that tables and content are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Confirm that the output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness and instruction compliance.
- Verify that repeated content from the instructions is minimized unless explicitly required.
- Ensure that redundant classifications, tags, and validations are avoided.
- Confirm that the output structure improves readability and review efficiency as per the instructions.
