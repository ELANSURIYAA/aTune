Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks

- Verify that all mandatory sections specified in the agent instructions are present in the output.
- Confirm that all required fields and blocks from the expected JSON output template are included.
- Ensure that all required analyses, such as mappingResults, entityScores, and aiInsights, are covered.
- Check that all entities, tables, and components referenced in the instructions are addressed in the output.
- Validate that all dependencies and integrations described in the instructions are captured.
- Confirm that all business rules and processing instructions from the agent instructions are reflected in the output.
- Ensure that all requested validations, such as dataQualityCheckRules, are present for each mapping entry.
- Verify that all use cases and scenarios described in the instructions are covered.
- Confirm that all required tables, headers, classifications, tags, and statuses are included as per the instructions.
- Ensure that all gap/missing item handling requirements from the instructions are addressed.
- Check that all formatting and structural requirements from the instructions are followed.
- Verify that no requested section from the instructions is skipped.
- Confirm that all iteration/resolution tracking requirements (if mentioned in the instructions) are included.
- Ensure that all referenced files/content in the instructions are analyzed where required.
- Validate that all mandatory mappings and relationships described in the instructions are included.
- Confirm that all required inferred or derived fields are generated if instructed.
- Note that no input file was found; ensure the output does not reference or fabricate content from missing input files.

Accuracy Checks

- Verify that the output aligns strictly with the provided agent instructions and expected output template.
- Confirm that no hallucinated content or unsupported assumptions are introduced in the output.
- Ensure that dependency and technical mappings are logically correct as per the instructions.
- Validate that gap identification and status assignments are justified by the instructions.
- Confirm that risk/severity classifications, if present, are appropriate and instruction-aligned.
- Ensure that inferred content is explicitly tagged if required by the instructions.
- Check that all findings and output fields are traceable to the instructions.
- Confirm that no contradictions exist across sections of the output.
- Validate that business rule interpretations are correct and consistent with the instructions.
- Ensure that terminology is used consistently and matches the instructions.
- Confirm that required formatting standards are followed exactly as specified.
- Ensure that no false-positive gaps or false-negative omissions occur in the output.
- Validate that values, tables, and classifications accurately match the instruction requirements.
- Confirm that relationships between entities and components are correctly interpreted per the instructions.
- Ensure that status and resolution tracking is accurate if applicable.
- Confirm that recommendations (if any) align with the identified findings and instructions.
- Ensure that no instruction is misinterpreted or partially implemented.
- Confirm that the output does not reference or fabricate content from missing input files.

Efficiency Checks

- Verify that duplicate analysis and redundant explanations are avoided in the output.
- Ensure that repeated gap entries and duplicate dependency mappings are not present.
- Confirm that the output remains concise, structured, and free of unnecessary sections.
- Ensure that formatting is clean and optimized for reviewer efficiency.
- Confirm that excessive verbosity and overlapping findings are avoided.
- Ensure that tables and output sections are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Validate that the output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness.
- Confirm that repeated content from the instructions is minimized unless necessary.
- Ensure that similar findings are grouped logically where appropriate.
- Confirm that redundant classifications, tags, and validations are avoided.
- Ensure that duplicate validations and checks are eliminated.
- Validate that large sections are summarized efficiently where allowed by the instructions.
- Confirm that the output structure improves readability and review efficiency.
- Ensure that the output does not include unnecessary references to missing input files.
