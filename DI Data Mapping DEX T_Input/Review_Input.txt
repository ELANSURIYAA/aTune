Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks

- Verify that all mandatory sections specified in the agent instructions are present in the output.
- Confirm that all required fields and blocks from the expected JSON output template are included.
- Ensure that all required analyses, such as mappingResults, entityScores, and aiInsights, are covered.
- Check that all entities, tables, and components referenced in the instructions are addressed in the output.
- Validate that all dependencies and integrations mentioned in the instructions are captured.
- Confirm that all business rules and processing instructions from the agent instructions are reflected in the output.
- Ensure that all requested validations, such as dataQualityCheckRules, are present for each mapping.
- Verify that all use cases and scenarios described in the instructions are covered.
- Confirm that all required tables, headers, and classifications/tags/statuses are included as per the instructions.
- Ensure that all gap/missing item handling requirements from the instructions are addressed.
- Validate that all formatting requirements specified in the instructions are followed.
- Confirm that no requested section from the instructions is skipped.
- Ensure that all iteration/resolution tracking requirements, if mentioned, are included.
- Verify that all referenced files/content in the instructions are analyzed where required.
- Confirm that all mandatory mappings and relationships are included as per the instructions.
- Ensure that all required inferred or derived fields are generated if instructed.
- Validate that the output structure matches the strict JSON format required by the instructions.

Accuracy Checks

- Verify that the output aligns strictly with the provided agent instructions.
- Confirm that no hallucinated content or unsupported assumptions are introduced in the output.
- Ensure that dependency and technical mappings are logically and technically correct as per the instructions.
- Validate that gap identification and risk/severity classifications are justified by the instructions.
- Confirm that inferred content is explicitly tagged if required by the instructions.
- Ensure that all findings are traceable to the agent instructions.
- Check that there are no contradictions across sections of the output.
- Validate that business rule interpretations are correct and consistent with the instructions.
- Confirm that terminology is used consistently and matches the instructions.
- Ensure that required formatting standards from the instructions are followed correctly.
- Validate that no false-positive gaps or false-negative omissions occur based on the instructions.
- Confirm that values, tables, and classifications accurately match the requirements in the instructions.
- Ensure that relationships between entities and components are correctly interpreted as per the instructions.
- Validate that status and resolution tracking is accurate if applicable.
- Confirm that recommendations align with the identified findings in the instructions.
- Ensure that no instruction is misinterpreted or partially implemented.
- Validate that the output contains no content outside the required JSON object.

Efficiency Checks

- Verify that duplicate analysis and redundant explanations are avoided in the output.
- Ensure that repeated gap entries and duplicate dependency mappings are eliminated.
- Confirm that the output remains concise, structured, and free of unnecessary sections.
- Validate that formatting remains clean and optimized for review.
- Ensure that excessive verbosity and overlapping findings are avoided.
- Confirm that tables and content are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Validate that the output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness.
- Ensure that repeated content from the instructions is minimized unless necessary.
- Confirm that similar findings are grouped logically where appropriate.
- Validate that redundant classifications, tags, and validations are avoided.
- Ensure that large sections are summarized efficiently where allowed by the instructions.
- Confirm that the output structure improves readability and review efficiency.
- Ensure that the output is efficient even in the absence of input files, focusing only on instruction compliance.
