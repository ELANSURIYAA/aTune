Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks

- Verify that all mandatory sections specified in the agent instructions are present in the output.
- Confirm that all required fields and blocks from the expected JSON output template are included.
- Ensure that all required analyses, such as mappingResults, entityScores, and aiInsights, are covered.
- Check that all entities, tables, and components referenced in the instructions are addressed, even if no input file was provided.
- Validate that all dependencies, integrations, and business rules from the instructions are reflected in the output.
- Confirm that all requested validations, use cases, and scenarios from the instructions are present.
- Ensure that all mandatory headers, classifications, tags, and statuses are included as per the instructions.
- Verify that gap/missing item handling requirements are covered, especially in the absence of input files.
- Confirm that all formatting requirements from the instructions are followed.
- Ensure that no requested section from the instructions is skipped or omitted.

Accuracy Checks

- Confirm that the output aligns strictly with the provided agent instructions and expected output template.
- Ensure that no hallucinated content or unsupported assumptions are introduced in the absence of input files.
- Validate that dependency mappings, technical mappings, and business rule interpretations are logically correct as per instructions.
- Check that all findings, values, tables, and classifications accurately match the requirements in the instructions.
- Ensure that all terminology and formatting standards are used consistently and correctly.
- Confirm that no contradictions exist across sections of the output.
- Verify that all required fields are populated and justified as per the expected output.
- Ensure that no instruction is misinterpreted or partially implemented.
- Confirm that the output correctly reflects the absence of input files, if applicable, without fabricating data.

Efficiency Checks

- Verify that duplicate analysis, redundant explanations, and repeated gap entries are avoided.
- Ensure that the output remains concise, structured, and free of unnecessary sections.
- Confirm that duplicate dependency mappings, risks, recommendations, and validations are eliminated.
- Check that formatting is clean, optimized, and avoids excessive verbosity.
- Ensure that overlapping findings are consolidated and similar findings are grouped logically where appropriate.
- Confirm that tables and content are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Validate that the output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness.
- Ensure that large sections are summarized efficiently where allowed by the instructions.
- Confirm that the output structure improves readability and review efficiency.
