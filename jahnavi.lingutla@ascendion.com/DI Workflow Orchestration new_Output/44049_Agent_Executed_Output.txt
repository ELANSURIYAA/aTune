| Criteria            | Description                          |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Self-validation     | **Requirements Parsing & Validation Agent**: Measure completeness coverage rate (target: 100%) by cross-referencing parsed sections against a predefined checklist of mandatory sections (business goals, entities, attributes, relationships, business rules, compliance requirements); re-validate parsed output against source document to detect parsing errors before handoff. **Entity & Attribute Modeling Agent**: Measure entity coverage rate (target: 100%) by cross-referencing defined entities against requirements; validate all relationships for correct cardinality and optionality; re-check attribute completeness (all mandatory attributes defined) before handoff. **Business Rules & Compliance Agent**: Measure rule coverage rate (target: 100%) by cross-referencing extracted rules against requirements; validate all compliance requirements against regulatory standards checklist; re-check audit trail specifications for completeness before handoff. **Model Synthesis & Integration Agent**: Measure model completeness (target: 100%) by validating all entities, attributes, relationships, and rules are integrated; cross-reference final model against all five reporting objectives to ensure full coverage; re-validate governance approval status before handoff. |
| Boundary definition | **Requirements Parsing & Validation Agent**: Must NOT alter or invent requirements; must NOT proceed with modeling if critical sections are missing; must NOT assume business rules or compliance requirements without explicit documentation; must escalate any ambiguity or missing section. **Entity & Attribute Modeling Agent**: Must NOT invent entities without evidence in requirements; must NOT omit mandatory attributes; must NOT assume relationships without explicit requirements; must NOT proceed with incomplete entity definitions; must escalate all ambiguity. **Business Rules & Compliance Agent**: Must NOT omit compliance requirements; must NOT invent business rules; must NOT approve incomplete documentation; must NOT bypass compliance validation; must escalate all compliance uncertainty. **Model Synthesis & Integration Agent**: Must NOT hand off incomplete model; must NOT bypass validation; must NOT omit integration steps; must NOT proceed without governance approval; must escalate all integration/validation failures. |
| Known issues log    | **Requirements Parsing & Validation Agent**: Log parsing failures (document format not supported, section headers not recognized), false positives in gap detection (section present but not detected), escalation delays (stakeholder unavailable for gap resolution), token limit exceeded (document too large). **Entity & Attribute Modeling Agent**: Log entity ambiguity patterns (unclear entity boundaries, overlapping entity definitions), relationship cardinality errors (incorrect one-to-many vs many-to-many), attribute completeness failures (mandatory attributes missing), escalation delays (domain expert unavailable). **Business Rules & Compliance Agent**: Log compliance requirement omissions (regulatory standard not checked), conflicting business rules (contradictory calculation logic), incomplete audit specifications (missing audit log fields), escalation delays (compliance officer unavailable). **Model Synthesis & Integration Agent**: Log integration failures (governance API unavailable, validation errors not resolved), incomplete model components (missing entity specifications, missing rules catalog), validation cycle timeouts (validation taking too long), governance approval delays (approval workflow stuck). |
