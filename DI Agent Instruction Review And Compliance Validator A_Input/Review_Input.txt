Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks

- Verify that all mandatory sections from the instructions are present in the agent output.
- Confirm that all required fields and columns specified in the instructions are included.
- Ensure all requested analyses and validations are covered.
- Check that all entities/files/components mentioned in the instructions are analyzed.
- Validate that all dependencies and integrations referenced in the instructions are addressed.
- Confirm that all business rules from the instructions are covered.
- Ensure all requested use cases/scenarios are included.
- Verify that all required tables and headers are generated.
- Confirm all required classifications, tags, and statuses are included.
- Ensure gap/missing item handling requirements are followed.
- Validate that all formatting requirements from the instructions are adhered to.
- Confirm that no requested section is skipped.
- Ensure all referenced files/content are analyzed where required.
- Verify that all mandatory mappings and relationships are included.
- Confirm that all required inferred or derived fields are generated if instructed.
- Ensure all iteration/resolution tracking requirements are included if mentioned.
- Validate that all input expectations and output expectations are addressed.

Accuracy Checks

- Verify that the output aligns with the provided instructions.
- Confirm that the output correctly reflects the instruction content.
- Ensure no hallucinated content exists in the output.
- Validate that no unsupported assumptions are introduced.
- Check that dependency mappings are logically correct.
- Confirm that technical mappings are valid.
- Ensure gap identification is justified by the instruction content.
- Validate that risk/severity classifications are appropriate.
- Confirm that inferred content is explicitly tagged if required.
- Ensure findings are traceable to the instructions.
- Validate that no contradictions exist across sections.
- Confirm business rule interpretations are correct.
- Ensure terminology is used consistently.
- Validate that required formatting standards are followed correctly.
- Confirm that no false-positive gaps are generated.
- Ensure no false-negative omissions occur.
- Validate that values, tables, and classifications accurately match the instruction content.
- Confirm relationships between entities/components are correctly interpreted.
- Ensure status/resolution tracking is accurate if applicable.
- Validate that recommendations align with the identified findings.
- Confirm that data extracted from the instructions is represented correctly.
- Ensure no instruction is misinterpreted or partially implemented.

Efficiency Checks

- Verify that duplicate analysis is avoided.
- Confirm that redundant explanations are avoided.
- Ensure repeated gap entries are avoided.
- Validate that output remains concise and structured.
- Confirm that unnecessary sections are not generated.
- Ensure duplicate dependency mappings are avoided.
- Validate that repeated risks and recommendations are eliminated.
- Confirm that formatting remains clean and optimized.
- Ensure excessive verbosity is avoided.
- Validate that overlapping findings are consolidated.
- Confirm that tables are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Ensure output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness.
- Validate that repeated content from the instructions is minimized unless necessary.
- Confirm that similar findings are grouped logically where appropriate.
- Ensure redundant classifications and tags are avoided.
- Validate that duplicate validations and checks are eliminated.
- Confirm that large sections are summarized efficiently where allowed.
- Ensure output structure improves readability and review efficiency.
