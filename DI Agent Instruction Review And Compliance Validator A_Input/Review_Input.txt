Reviewer Validation Checklist

Completeness Checks
- Verify that all mandatory sections from the instructions are present in the agent output.
- Confirm that all required fields and columns specified in the instructions are included.
- Check that all requested analyses and validations are covered.
- Ensure all entities/files/components mentioned in the instructions are analyzed.
- Validate that all dependencies and integrations referenced in the instructions are addressed.
- Confirm all business rules from the instructions are covered.
- Ensure all formatting requirements from the instructions are followed.
- Verify that no requested section is skipped.
- Confirm all referenced files/content are analyzed where required.
- Ensure all mandatory mappings/relationships are included.
- Validate that all required inferred/derived fields are generated if instructed.

Accuracy Checks
- Confirm that the output aligns with the provided instructions.
- Verify that no hallucinated content exists in the output.
- Ensure no unsupported assumptions are introduced.
- Validate that dependency mappings are logically correct.
- Confirm that gap identification is justified by the instructions.
- Ensure risk/severity classifications are appropriate if present.
- Validate that findings are traceable to the instructions.
- Confirm no contradictions exist across sections.
- Ensure business rule interpretations are correct.
- Validate that terminology is used consistently.
- Confirm required formatting standards are followed correctly.
- Ensure no false-positive gaps are generated.
- Ensure no false-negative omissions occur.
- Validate that values/tables/classifications accurately match the instructions.
- Confirm relationships between entities/components are correctly interpreted.
- Ensure status/resolution tracking is accurate if applicable.
- Validate that recommendations align with the identified findings.
- Confirm data extracted from the instructions is represented correctly.
- Ensure no instruction is misinterpreted or partially implemented.

Efficiency Checks
- Verify that duplicate analysis is avoided.
- Confirm that redundant explanations are avoided.
- Ensure repeated gap entries are avoided.
- Validate that output remains concise and structured.
- Confirm unnecessary sections are not generated.
- Ensure duplicate dependency mappings are avoided.
- Validate that repeated risks/recommendations are eliminated.
- Confirm formatting remains clean and optimized.
- Ensure excessive verbosity is avoided.
- Validate that overlapping findings are consolidated.
- Confirm tables are not unnecessarily repeated.
- Ensure output is token-efficient while maintaining completeness.
- Validate that repeated content from the instructions is minimized unless necessary.
- Confirm similar findings are grouped logically where appropriate.
- Ensure redundant classifications/tags are avoided.
- Validate that duplicate validations/checks are eliminated.
- Confirm large sections are summarized efficiently where allowed.
- Ensure output structure improves readability and review efficiency.
